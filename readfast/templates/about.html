Made On An Island

A /dev/fort project

On the remote Isle of Eigg the /dev/fort Cohort 8 created The Reading Gym in just one week, in isolation.

For six days we lived and worked together in Glebe Barn, sharing design, product & development work, as well as household work like cooking and cleaning, on an island with only 100 inhabitants.
