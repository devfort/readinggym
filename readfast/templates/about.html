About The Reading Gym

A /dev/fort project

On the remote Isle of Eigg (which has only 100 inhabitants) the /dev/fort Cohort 8 created The Reading Gym in just one week, in isolation.

For six days we lived and worked together in Glebe Barn, sharing design, product & development work, as well as household work like cooking and cleaning.

What Else Happened?

Cohort 8 of /dev/fort was the largest group to date: 18 people ranging in profession, including designers, developers, writers, artists and an architect.

In total the cohort worked on five projects: The Reading Gym, Earth Lens, Look Around You, Check Your Font and a series of videos about the Isle of Eigg itself. The other members of Cohort 8 were:
